Dear friends,

It is with great honour and conviction I publicly declare my intent to seek the nomination to become the Conservative Party Candidate for New-Westminster - Burnaby. I do not solely want to be your Candidate; I want to be your Member of Parliament. But to get there, I need your help.

Born and bred in BC, I am a proud resident of New Westminster. I have been active in my community, working and living in New West and North Burnaby since 2009.

I am poised and ready to fight for our community's beliefs and values in the House of Commons. As your Candidate and future MP I will engage you, the community members; I will celebrate the cultural diversity that makes NWB unique and I will deliver on promises. Credibility and accountability go hand in hand and I believe there is a time in every persons life when one must step up for one’s values, neighbours, and community.

It is my time to demonstrate my abilities and rally support for you, for our cause, for our community.

I cannot do this alone. I need your support! I encourage you to join the party and get involved. There will be a nomination contest where your votes will decide who becomes your Candidate. It is your right to attend the Nomination Meeting and I highly encourage you to do so; the date and time of this meeting will be announced when the Nomination has been officially called.

Your support and involvement is essential to our success this coming election. It is my duty to show you the value in this process. It is my honour to show you how your individual contribution, no matter how small, can make a BIG difference.

I affirm that I will be accessible to you; I will be in the grocery store, on the board walk, at local events and around the community talking and listening to you.

I am committed to becoming your Candidate and your future MP for New Westminster - Burnaby. This is the time for us to come together and be the change we have craved for so long.

Join me. Vote Chloé Ellis. Because together, we can make a difference.


Kind regards,

BIOGRAPHY

Chloé Ellis was born in Vancouver, raised in Port Coquitlam and resides in New Westminster. After graduating from Riverside Secondary School, she continued her studies in business and marketing at Langara College.

While balancing a full time work schedule and a full time curriculum, she earned a Diploma in Business Management in. A year later, in 2012, she graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing Management from Langara College.

In 2013 Volume Studio hired her on as a part-time receptionist, and only one month later she was promoted to Manager. During this time Chloé pursued a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Northern BC (where Hon. James Moore, MP is a notable alumni) graduating as the youngest individual in her class.

Since 2009 Chloé has been actively involved in the Universities’ Model Parliament Society of BC. As a new comer to the society Chloé began as a CPC Party Representative and charged with recruitment and public awareness. In 2011 Chloé became the Vice President of the Society and worked to develop the Society’s first Sexual Harassment Policy and first Social Media Policy. At the 2015 AGM, both of these policies were officially adopted and forged in the Society’s Constitution.

In 2013, Chloé continued to make Society history when she was elected as both the UMP CPC Party Leader, (earning the title of first female Conservative Prime Minister elected in the Society’s forty-one year history,) as well as President of the Society.

She was elected to a rare second term in 2014. She now acts as Past President and enjoys an active advisory role within the Society.

Chloé Ellis Named as the Conservative Candidate in New Westminster – Burnaby

New Westminster: Alysia MacGrotty, Chair of the New Westminster – Burnaby Conservative Candidate Nomination Committee, announced today that Chloé Ellis has been named as the Conservative Party of Canada candidate in the riding of New Westminster – Burnaby.

A five-year resident and homeowner in New Westminster, Chloé Ellis is committed to representing her riding’s interests in Ottawa. Ms. Ellis is currently a Business Development Manager in Vancouver. She actively supports the promotion and development of small businesses and works hard to ensure their success. Ms. Ellis proudly supports the New Westminster Hyack Association and actively served on the Burnaby – New Westminster EDA as the Financial Agent.

“I am grateful and humbled to be named as the Conservative candidate in New Westminster – Burnaby,” stated Chloé Ellis. “I want to thank the Conservative party and its members in my riding for showing such resounding support.”

“The stakes are high in the next election for New Westminster – Burnaby, and our choice will clearly be between the strong, serious leadership of Prime Minister Stephen Harper, and the high risk, high tax and high debt agenda of Justin Trudeau and Thomas Mulcair,” Ms. Ellis added.
